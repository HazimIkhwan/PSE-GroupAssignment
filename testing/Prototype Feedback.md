# Testing Method
The prototype has been tested by interacting with the clickable screens of the Figma prototype. The testing has been done to ensure that each button and navigation element directs the user to the appropriate screen. This has enabled the team to assess the logical flow and ease of use of the application. The testing has been done on the following navigation flows within the system:

Scenario 1: User Login Navigation
- User clicks the "Sign In" button
- System navigates to the Home Dashboard

Scenario 2: Health Tracking Navigation
- User clicks the "Track" button in the navigation bar
- System navigates to the Health Tracking screen

Scenario 3: Appointment Booking Navigation
- User clicks the "Book" section
- System displays the doctor selection and appointment booking interface
  
Scenario 4: Appointment Cancellation
- User selects an appointment from the appointment history
- User clicks the cancel button
- System navigates to the cancellation confirmation screen

Scenario 5: Rewards Navigation
- User clicks the Rewards tab
- System displays achievements and voucher redemption options

# Google Forms results

 From the tesults obtained from the google forms, there was additional prototype feedback has been attained and can be summarised.

As the form's questions are based around the Product Vision document's proposed values and goals, the performance of the prototype can therefore be measured.

In terms of the Proposed Values:
- 50% of the respondents strongly agree that the app encourages daily physical activity.
- 50% of the respondents strongly agree that the app gives hydration awareness.
- 66.7% of respondents strongly agree the app gives users more motivation through gamification.
- 33% of the respondents agree while 33% of the respondents strongly agree that the app raises awareness about body health metrics (BMI).
- 33% of the respondents strongly agree while 33% of the respondents disagree that the app provides easier access to medical appointments to support timely health care.

In terms of the Product's Goals:
- 66.7% of the respondents strongly agree that the app gives student motivation to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
- 66.7% of the respondents strongly agree that the app app provides simple and accessible health tracking tools.
- 33% of the respondents agree while 33% of the respondents strongly agree the app encourages preventive health awareness.
- 83.3% of the respondents strongly agree that the app delivers an easy-to-use and user-friendly interface.
- 50% of the respondents strongly agree the app provides a convenient way for students to book clinic appointments.

As for Personal Feedbacks there are some notable answers such as:
"Overall, I think the app is useful, but I think the appointment making system is a bit lacking."

Overall, based on the Forms results it shows that on some aspects the app perfomrs well, but in others it performs rather averagely. Thus using the prototype feedback improvements can then be made.
